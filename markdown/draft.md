# IELTS Writing Task 1 - Sample Answer

## Consumption of Fast Food by Australian Teenagers (1975-2000)

### Introduction
The line graph illustrates the weekly consumption of three different types of fast food among teenagers in Australia over a 25-year period from 1975 to 2000.

### Overview
Overall, it is evident that pizza and hamburgers became increasingly popular among Australian teenagers, while fish and chips experienced a significant decline in consumption during this period.

### Body Paragraph 1 - Increasing Trends
Looking at the details, the number of pizzas consumed per week showed a dramatic increase, rising from approximately 5 in 1975 to around 82 in 2000. Similarly, hamburger consumption also climbed steadily, starting at roughly 10 items per week in 1975 and reaching approximately 100 by the year 2000, making it the most popular fast food item by the end of the period.

### Body Paragraph 2 - Decreasing Trend
By contrast, fish and chips, which was initially the most commonly consumed fast food in 1975 with around 100 items per week, witnessed a notable decrease to approximately 40 items per week in 2000. This represents a substantial drop of about 60% over the 25-year period.

### Conclusion
In conclusion, the graph clearly demonstrates a shift in teenage preferences from traditional fish and chips towards pizza and hamburgers, reflecting changing dietary habits among Australian adolescents during the late 20th century.

## Useful IELTS Task 1 Expressions:

### Describing Trends:
- **Increasing**: rose to, climbed to, increased to, grew to, surged to
- **Decreasing**: fell to, declined to, dropped to, decreased to, plummeted to
- **Stable**: remained stable, stayed constant, leveled off at

### Approximation:
- approximately, roughly, about, around, nearly, just over, just under

### Time References:
- over a ...-year period, during this time, from...to..., by the year...
